My name is Facundo Torres. I was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, in 1989. I am a visual artist, musician, and lawyer. My training is eclectic, primarily self-taught, drawing from the techniques of classical drawing and painting academies.

Art is the way I communicate what I cannot put into words, the space that heals what I did not know was damaged, and the path of learning toward what I only intuit.

Since 2022 my work has been exhibited across Cordoba province, in government institutions, cultural centers, and public museums. In parallel, I develop institutional projects where visual art meets education, artificial intelligence, and neurotechnology.

For inquiries: WhatsApp or email.

I offer drawing courses both in-person and online. Each session is focused on technique, expression, and developing a personal visual language.
